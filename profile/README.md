We are an independent Brazilian research lab developing open-source language models that understand the language, culture, and regulatory context of Brazil natively — without relying on translation layers or foreign APIs. Our work contributes to the [Brazilian AI Plan (PBIA 2024–2028)](https://www.gov.br/mcti/pt-br/acompanhe-o-mcti/transformacaodigital/plano-brasileiro-de-inteligencia-artificial).

### Projects

**[maracatu](https://github.com/maracatu-ai/maracatu)** — flagship LLM. Llama-style decoder-only transformer trained from scratch on Brazilian Portuguese. Apache 2.0.

**[calunga](https://github.com/maracatu-ai/calunga)** — RAG system for transparency in Brazilian public spending, querying official sources with citations.

### Naming

Our projects are named after elements of the *Maracatu*, an Afro-Brazilian cultural tradition from Pernambuco recognized as intangible heritage by UNESCO.
